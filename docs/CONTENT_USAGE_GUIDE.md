# Content Usage Guide

## Purpose

This guide explains how to apply content from `docs/CONTENT_COLLECTION.md` to Day 1 pages consistently.

## Rules

1. **No empty public pages** at Day 1 launch.
2. Prefer short, clear copy over long narrative walls.
3. Maintain factual accuracy; do not invent biographical claims.
4. Keep tone respectful and mission-forward.

## Page Mapping (Day 1)

### Home
- Mission statement
- One-sentence bio
- Weekly matchup entry point
- Clear join/login CTA

### About
- Short paragraph bio
- Mission expansion
- “Why this exists” section

### Join
- Membership explanation
- What members get (Day 1)
- What is planned (Day 2/3) — keep aspirational language minimal and labeled

### Milestones
- Minimal timeline seeds (expand later)

### FAQ
- Use FAQ seeds and refine answers for clarity

## Editing Guidelines

- Keep headlines short
- Keep paragraphs under ~4 lines where possible
- Use bullets for lists and features
- Avoid duplicate copy across pages (reuse ideas, not verbatim text)
